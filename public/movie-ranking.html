Varag's Favourite Horror Movies

My top 3 Horror movies of all time

The Shining

"The Shining," directed by Stanley Kubrick and based on Stephen King's novel, is a psychological horror film renowned for its eerie atmosphere and iconic scenes. It delves into themes of isolation, madness, and supernatural forces as it follows a family's chilling experiences at the remote Overlook Hotel during the winter season.

The Insidious 2010

"Insidious," directed by James Wan, is a supernatural horror film that explores the haunting of a family by malevolent spirits. Known for its suspenseful atmosphere and effective jump scares, the movie blends psychological terror with supernatural elements, creating a gripping narrative that delves into the realms of the afterlife and astral projection.

The Exorcist

"The Exorcist," directed by William Friedkin and based on William Peter Blatty's novel, is a seminal horror film known for its terrifying portrayal of demonic possession. Released in 1973, it shocked audiences with its intense scenes and unsettling imagery, becoming a cultural phenomenon. The film's exploration of faith, doubt, and the battle between good and evil remains influential in the horror genre to this day.
